Biography

Gang Chen received B.Eng. and M.Eng in Mechanical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from the University of California, Davis. He is currently a research fellow with the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.

Research Interests

  • Signal Processing, Time-Frequency Analysis.
  • Formal Methods, Control Theory.
  • Machine Learning, Monitoring and Control.
